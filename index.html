ABOUT D11 ARCHITECTURE

Precision in Every Line

D11 Architecture specializes in creating detailed house plans, building designs, and technical drawings for residential and commercial construction projects.

From initial concept sketches to complete construction documents, we provide professional CAD drafting services including floor plans, elevations, sections, interior layouts, and exterior designs. With 15+ years of experience and 250+ completed projects, we deliver accurate, permit-ready drawings that bring your vision to reality.

Our Design Process

1. Discovery & Concept

Deep dive into client needs, site analysis, and defining the core design vision, mood boards, and initial sketches.

2. Schematic Design

Developing floor plans, spatial layouts, elevations, and exploring primary material palettes and key furniture concepts.

3. Detailed Documentation

Finalizing construction drawings, sourcing specifications, detailed lighting plans, and securing all necessary permits.

4. Execution & Installation

Managing the construction process, site supervision, bespoke fabrication, and final installation and styling.

5. Project Handover

Final walk-through, quality check, and presenting the completed, curated space to the client.
